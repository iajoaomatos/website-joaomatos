# **PROMPTS PARA WEBISTE**

\# HERO SECTION

Build ONLY the Hero Section.

Do not generate any other section.

Follow all previous project documents.

\------------------------------------------------------------

GOAL

The Hero Section has one purpose only:

make visitors immediately understand:

WHO

\+

WHAT

\+

WHY

within 15 seconds.

\------------------------------------------------------------

IMPORTANT

Avoid introducing João as:

\- Digital Marketer  
\- Product Owner  
\- UX Researcher  
\- Product Manager  
\- Analytics Professional

Those are disciplines and responsibilities.

They are not João's positioning.

\------------------------------------------------------------

POSITIONING

Communicate João as someone that helps organisations:

\- build better digital products  
\- make better digital decisions  
\- understand people, products and performance

His multidisciplinary background should feel natural and subtle.

Never overwhelm visitors with too many disciplines or technologies.

\------------------------------------------------------------

THE RULE OF THREE

Visitors should remember only three ideas.

01

Product.

02

Analytics.

03

Better Decisions.

Everything else is secondary.

\------------------------------------------------------------

WRITING STYLE

Keep copy extremely short.

Never create:

large paragraphs.

Prefer:

one great sentence.

instead of

five average ones.

\------------------------------------------------------------

READING TIME

Maximum:

15 seconds.

\------------------------------------------------------------

HERO STRUCTURE

Headline

\+

Subheadline

\+

Small supporting sentence

\+

Profile Image (align right)

\+

2 Call To Actions

\------------------------------------------------------------

EXAMPLES

GOOD

Helping organisations make better digital decisions through Product Thinking, Analytics and Marketing.

GOOD

Connecting people, products and data to create measurable outcomes.

GOOD

Building better digital experiences through curiosity, analytics and continuous learning.

BAD

13+ years of experience in Digital Marketing, Product Management, UX Research, Product Analytics, CRO, Digital Transformation and...

Never do this.

\------------------------------------------------------------

MICRO INTERACTIONS

Keep animations subtle, but modern and tech.

Prefer:

fade

small transitions

soft movements.

Avoid:

complex animations.

\------------------------------------------------------------

CTA

Maximum two.

Examples:

Explore my work.

and

Continue reading.

or

Let's talk.

\------------------------------------------------------------

VERY IMPORTANT

Do not attempt to explain João's entire professional journey here.

Create curiosity.

Visitors should naturally want to continue scrolling.

\------------------------------------------------------------

MAIN OBJECTIVE

The Hero Section should make visitors think:

"I understand what this professional does and I want to know more."

\------------------------------------------------------------

FINAL PRINCIPLE

Less is more.

If one sentence can replace five paragraphs,

always prefer the sentence.
