# **PROMPTS PARA WEBSITE**

\# LET'S TALK

Build ONLY this section.

Do not create any other section.

Follow all previous project documents.

\------------------------------------------------------------

GOAL

This section has one purpose only.

Create a natural and human invitation to start a conversation.

Visitors should feel invited to connect professionally and continue the conversation.

\------------------------------------------------------------

IMPORTANT

This section is NOT:

\- a traditional Contact page  
\- a services page  
\- a lead generation page  
\- a complex contact form

Avoid:

\- multiple call-to-actions  
\- multiple buttons  
\- large forms  
\- unnecessary information

Keep everything simple.

\------------------------------------------------------------

READING TIME

Maximum:

10 seconds.

\------------------------------------------------------------

CONTENT STRATEGY

Communicate only three ideas:

01

Curiosity.

02

Conversation.

03

Connection.

Everything else is secondary.

\------------------------------------------------------------

WRITING STYLE

Professional.

Friendly.

Human.

Minimal.

Avoid:

corporate language.

Examples:

GOOD

If anything you've read resonates with you, let's continue the conversation.

GOOD

Whether it's Product, Analytics or simply exchanging ideas, I'm always happy to connect with curious people.

GOOD

The best ideas usually start with a simple conversation.

BAD

Let's schedule a discovery call.

BAD

Contact me today.

BAD

Looking for consulting services?

Avoid sounding commercial.

\------------------------------------------------------------

DESIGN PRINCIPLES

Minimalism first.

Prefer:

\- lots of whitespace  
\- one elegant CTA  
\- subtle interactions  
\- calm transitions

Avoid:

\- multiple buttons  
\- complex layouts  
\- large forms  
\- distracting animations

\------------------------------------------------------------

CONTACT OPTIONS

Maximum:

three options.

Examples:

\- Linkedin  
\- CTA to open the mailto: Email  
\- Main Website

Medium may be included only if it complements the overall layout.

Never overload this section.

\------------------------------------------------------------

CALL TO ACTION

Maximum:

ONE primary CTA.

Examples:

Let's Talk.

or

Continue the conversation.

or

Get in touch.

Prefer one excellent CTA instead of multiple alternatives.

\------------------------------------------------------------

MICRO INTERACTIONS

Prefer:

small hover effects

small transitions

elegant animations

\------------------------------------------------------------

VERY IMPORTANT

Visitors should never feel like they are entering a sales funnel.

They should feel invited to start a professional conversation.

\------------------------------------------------------------

MAIN OBJECTIVE

After reading this section visitors should think:

"This feels like the beginning of a conversation rather than the end of a website."

\------------------------------------------------------------

FINAL PRINCIPLE

People connect with people.

Keep everything human.

Less is always more.
