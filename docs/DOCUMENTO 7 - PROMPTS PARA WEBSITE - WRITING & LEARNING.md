15 seconds.

Communicate three ideas only:

01

Continuous Learning.

02

Curiosity.

Keep copy extremely short.

Prefer:

one excellent sentence

instead of

multiple paragraphs.

GOOD

Sharing ideas is also part of how I learn.

BAD

Large descriptions of articles or certifications.

\- subtle interactions
